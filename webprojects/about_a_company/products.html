Latest Released Games

Here is a list of all the Latest Monster Hunter games and where to buy them!

  Generations Ultimate

Mosnter Hunter Generations Ultimate is an expansion on Monster Hunter Generations. It includes new game features and monsters, and even introduces the elder dragon Valstrax.

  World

Monster Hunter World is widly reconized as being the best Monster Hunter game. It follows the Hunters Guild as they explore the New World and research why the elder dragons cross the sea every 10 years.

  Rise

Monster Hunter Rise is the second Monster Hunter game to release for the Nintindo Switch (the first being Generations Ultimate). It follows the hunter of Kamura, a small village on a remote island. The village is preparing to fend off the Rampage, where local monsters start sieging the village.

  Wilds

Monster Hunter Wilds is the most recent Monster Hunter game to have been released.It follows the hunters journying to the Forbidden Lands to find the White Wraith and investigate its detremental impact on the enviornment.
