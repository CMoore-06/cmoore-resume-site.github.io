A Brief History

The Monster Hunter franchise is a series of games developed by Capcom. The first game was released in March of 2004 in Japan, and an American release in the following September, followed by a European release in 2005. Since then the Monster Hunter franchise has become wildly sucessful and now has a total of over 25 games (including spin-offs, moble games, and dlcs and enhanced versions). The game most recently released was Monster Hunter Wilds.
